
Privacy Policy for Lexa

Lexa does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

All text analysis is performed locally within the user's browser. No data is sent to external servers.

Lexa does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party data collection tools.

If future versions introduce features that require data storage or transmission, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

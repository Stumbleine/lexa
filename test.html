⚠️ Edge Cases & False Positives (Should NOT be highlighted)

Present Tense (NOT past simple)
❌ I need water right now. (Present simple, not past)
Nouns/Adjectives ending in 'ed'
❌ The red bed is comfortable. (Colors/nouns, not verbs)
'Have' as main verb (NOT present perfect)
❌ She has a new car. (Possession, not auxiliary)
Adjective (NOT present continuous)
❌ He is tired after work. (Adjective, not verb-ing)
Adjective (NOT future perfect)
❌ They will be happy with the results. (Adjective, not past participle)

🔬 Complex Sentences (Multiple Tenses Combined)

Mixed Tenses
While I was walking to work, I realized that I had forgotten my keys, so I will have to go back home.
Perfect Continuous Chain
She has been studying for three hours, and by 6 PM she will have been studying for five hours straight.
Conditional Past
If they had arrived earlier, they would have seen the opening ceremony.
